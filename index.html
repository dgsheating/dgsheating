Hi! I'm Daniel sare, a reliable, honest and prideful gas and heating engineer. I'm Gas Safe registered (591597), and do my best to please customers, both old and new. I aim to be your one stop shop. Not only do I focus on plumbing and pipe-work, I can also work on bathrooms, kitchens and general painting and decorating.

Keeping your home comfortable and safe is our family business. DGS Heating is a trusted, local gas & heating company based in Chelmsford, with a reputation for reliable service and expert technicians.

Whether you need installation, maintenance, or repair, our team of licensed and insured technicians are here to ensure your gas and heating system runs smoothly all year round. We offer a variety of services, including water heater installations, boiler maintenance and more!
